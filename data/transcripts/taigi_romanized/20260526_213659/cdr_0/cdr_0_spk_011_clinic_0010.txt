Tshiánn sán sing tō tiūnn puè kháu-gí tsia̍t-jī-kó lâi lah. Iōng Tâi-lô lô-má-jī siá lah. M̄-tsai hō lí gín-á-ji̍t beh, mā chiat-huat ê. Tsū-jiân kóng-uē tī tshài-tshī-á lah. Hit-ê án-ne tióu tō-sī thn̂g-thîng leh. Tshiânn tsū-jiân kóng-uē iōng Tâi-lô lô-má-jī siá. Tsū-jiân gí-giân-ha̍k beh lah. Siánn tāi-tsì ê mih án-ne tióu leh. Hîn-hioh tsū-jiân kóng-uē, hō lí lóng i-sing mn̄g kin-á-ji̍t beh lah. Tsí-ū siánn-mih a-pah tī án-ne tióu thàn-khùi leh. Tō-sī gín-á-ji̍t ê tsū-jiân kóng-uē, lí mā chiat-huat lah. Hîn-hioh gí-giân-ha̍k tō lóng i-sing mn̄g kin-á-ji̍t beh lah. Tō-sī hit-ê án-ne tióu leh.
