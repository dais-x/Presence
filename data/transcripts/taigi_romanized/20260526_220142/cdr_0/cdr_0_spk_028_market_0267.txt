Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, hit kò bē tshài-thâu a-pô tsin siān-sim, i kóng kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì tsiok-hó, tshài-thâu kè-tsînn bô piàn-tōng. Guá bé kuí-luā ia̍p ko-lê-tshài, nn̄g ke-nn̄g, tsi̍t kin ti-bah. <pause> thâu-ke-niû sǹg khah sio̍k, kóng guá ē-tshù láng lâi, i tsiok huann-hí. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing sé tshài, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú . Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, kóng a-má kin-á-ji̍t bé ê tshài-thâu siāng hó-tsia̍h. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
