Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á tâng sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian kóng-uē. A-bó tsún-pī tsi̍t tiánn sio thîng thîng ê pe̍h-pn̄g, koh ū-hó kuí puânn tshài-thâu, ko-lê-tshài, hî-bah. Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, sing mn̄g i a-má ū-bô tsia̍h-pn̄g? A-bó kóng m̄ tsia̍h, tán kiánn á tâng sun-á lâi tsâi ē tsia̍h. <pause> sun-á tio̍h tōo phuê--ah, kóng-hó gō--lah! Ta-ke uî tsē tī tsàu-kha pian, khai-sí tāng to sî tsia̍h-pn̄g. Tshài tsin tsiok tshenn, thng iū siāng-hó hiunn, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsia̍h tit huann-hí. A-bó tshiò tio̍h kóng, kin-á-ji̍t tsiok huann-hí tsi̍t thinn--ah!
