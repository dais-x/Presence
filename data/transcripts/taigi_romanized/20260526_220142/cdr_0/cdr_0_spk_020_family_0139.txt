Kin-á-ji̍t a-bó kiò kiánn á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, tī tsàu-kha pian kóng-uē. Sun-á sing-lâi--lah, i láng tsē tī tsí siāng, tsin huann-hí tán kiánn á. <pause> gín-á tńg--lâi--ah, i bé tsi̍t nâ tshài-thâu, kuí-luā ia̍p ko-lê-tshài, lîng lâi tshù-lāi. A-bó tsú tsia̍h, tsoh tsi̍t tiánn tshài-thâu thng, tsi̍t tiánn ti-bah kenn. Sun-á tsia̍h pn̄g-tsîng sing sé-uánn, tsin kuann tsīng. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á láng tsē tī tsàu-kha pian tsia̍h-pn̄g, kóng-uē siann kòng hué, tsiânn sio nāu.
