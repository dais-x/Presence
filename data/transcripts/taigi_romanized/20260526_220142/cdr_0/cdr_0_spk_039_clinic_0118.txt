Kin-á-ji̍t tsá-thâu, guá tsia̍h uánn ka-tshài. I tsin pá-tōo. Tńg--khì pēnn-īnn khuànn i-sing, i-sing mn̄g guá kuí sî tsia̍h-pn̄g? Guá kóng thàu-tsá tsia̍h--ah. Sun-á tso̍h--ji̍t huán ka, a-bó tsú tsi̍t tiánn thng kip i tsia̍h. Thng īng ke-nn̄g tâng ko-lê-tshài tsú, siāng hó-tsia̍h. I-sing mn̄g kin-ji̍t kuí hō, guá tap m̄ tsai, iau a-má tshâ li̍k tsu. <pause> suà-lo̍h huán tshù, sing sé-uánn, koh-khì tsàu-kha tsú pn̄g. Sun-á pàng-o̍h huán lâi, kóng i tsia̍h thng tsiok huann-hí. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin ko hing, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
