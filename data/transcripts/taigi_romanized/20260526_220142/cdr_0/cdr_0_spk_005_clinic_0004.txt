Kin-á-ji̍t sī jī gue̍h tsa̍p-sann, guá tsá á tsia̍h-pá pn̄g khì biō kháu bé tshài. Hit kò bē tshài a-peh tsin sio tsiânn, i kóng kin-ji̍t thinn-khì hó, bô lo̍h-hōo. Guá bé kuí ia̍p ko-lê-tshài, tsi̍t kin ti-bah, koh bé nn̄g ke-nn̄g. <pause> thâu-ke-niû koh sǹg khah sio̍k, kóng guá ta̍k-pái láng lâi, i huann-hí guá. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing sé-sann, suà--lo̍h-lâi pha̍k-sann. A-bó mn̄g guá tsá á tsia̍h siánn? Guá kóng tsia̍h tsi̍t tiánn tâng kuí ia̍p tshài-thâu. Sun-á tann tsá bô--khì ha̍k-hāu, in-uī i kám-mōo tio̍h. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin tam-sim, ē-sái kiò i to hiu sik, m̄ hó tshut-khì. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī pâng-king liâu-thian, tsiânn huann-hí.
