Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá, guá sing sé-bīn, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsia̍h-pn̄g. A-bó tsú nn̄g puânn-tshài, tsi̍t puânn tshài-thâu kiâm-tshài, tsi̍t puânn ko-lê-tshài bah kenn. Koh sing tsíng-lí tshù-lāi mi̍h-kiānn, siu láng luān tshit peh tsau tang-sai. Tńg--khì pâng-king, tshīng tio̍h tshut-mn̂g sann-khòo, tì kín bō tsí. <pause> suà--lo̍h-lâi ná-tshiú tsih, tsún-pī khì biō kháu pài-pài. Sun-á kóng sueh iau khì ha̍k-hāu, m̄-koh tī ka lí tsia̍h tsá-tshan sing. Guá kóng-hó--lah, tsia̍h-pá khì kiánn á tō bô tî-tò. Tńg--khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, thâu-ke-niû bē tshài tsin huann-hí. Kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì siōng hó, tshut ji̍t-thâu lâi tio̍h bē ū hōo, koh ē-sái khì kong-hn̂g sàn-pōo.
