Kin-á-ji̍t sī jī gue̍h tsa̍p tshit, thinn-khì hó, bô lo̍h-hōo--ah. Thàu-tsá guá khí-lâi tsia̍h-pn̄g, a-bó tsú tshài-thâu tàn thng kin tāu-hū kuann. Thng bī tsin-hó, pn̄g iā-bē pá, guá tsia̍h-kàu tsi̍t puànn, sun-á lâi kiò guá iau khì ha̍k-hāu. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, guá sé-uánn--lah, koh tsún-pī àm-sî ê mńg tsiok. A-má mn̄g guá tann tsá ū-bô tsia̍h siánn, guá kóng ū tsia̍h tshài-thâu tàn thng kin pn̄g--ah. Honn, tsin sī tsi̍t thinn khai-sí--lah.
