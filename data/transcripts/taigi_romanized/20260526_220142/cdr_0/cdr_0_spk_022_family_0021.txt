Kin-á-ji̍t sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, sing khì tshù-pinn guán kiánn á ka láng tsia̍h-pn̄g. Guán a-bó tsú tsi̍t tiánn sat-ba̍k-hî thng, tsin-hó bī. Sun-á kóng: "a-má tsú ê thng siāng hó-tsia̍h." <pause> guán bóo sueh: "ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian, lâi kóng-uē." guán kiánn á kin sun-á thiann-tio̍h, koh leh tshiò. Guán-ang kin a-bó iā láng lâi, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē kín-bi̍t. Guán kiánn á kóng tsuè-kīn kang-tsok siāng-hó bâng, bô sî-sîn khùn kak. Sun-á sueh ha̍k-hāu ua̍h-tāng tsin to, muí tsiu to tńg--khì tsham-ka. Ta-ke kóng kóng-tshiò tshiò, tsiânn huann-hí.
