Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá kiánn á tâng sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian kóng-uē. Sun-á kóng sueh ha̍k-hāu lí sin o̍h kàu tsi̍t-tsūn tsí kua-iâu, koh siūnn-tio̍h kiò a-bó thiann. <pause> sun-á tō tshiàng tsi̍t-tsūn tsí kip a-bó thiann, a-bó thiann liáu tsin huann-hí, sueh sun-á khí tshing tshè. Kiánn á iā mā lâi tah khiunn, kóng a-má tshiàng kua-siann hiáng tsiok hó-thiann. Guá thiann-tio̍h honn huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
