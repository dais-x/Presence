Kin-á-ji̍t àm-sî m̄ tsá tsia̍h tsá-tshan, guá tsú tsi̍t tiánn tâng kuí tè bah sang. tsin nńg, bah sang tsiok hiunn. Sun-á kóng hó-tsia̍h, leh tsia̍h hó huann-hí. Tńg--khì tsín-sóo khuànn i-sing, i mn̄g kin-ji̍t ji̍t-kî, tī--honnh tsa-hng. I-sing kóng kin-á-ji̍t sī tsa̍p-it gue̍h jia̍p gōo. <pause> guá kóng tsá-tshan tsia̍h-bô--lah, i-sing tō kóng m̄-koh bô kuainn kín-iàu, ē-tshù tsá-khí tsia̍h tō hó. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, a-bó bé tshài khì, guá pang-bâng sé-sann. Gōo-sî sun-á lâi thàm bāng, láng huann-hí.
