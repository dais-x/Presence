Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á tâng sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, guá sing tsú tsi̍t tiánn tshài-thâu thng. <pause> tsú hó āu, kiò ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian tsia̍h-pn̄g. A-kong kóng i tsia̍h bē pá, guá iū khì tsú kuí tè bah ka tsìn khì. Sun-á sueh tsin hó-tsia̍h, ta-ke to tsia̍h tit huann-hí. Tsia̍h uân pn̄g--lah, kiánn á tâng sun-á tō khì pâng-king tsò kong-khò, guá mā sé-uánn kuann tsīng. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tshù-lāi tsin sio nāu, bē ē tsi̍k-bo̍k.
