Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á kin sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, guá sing khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài. Hit kò bē hî a-peh kóng sat-ba̍k-hî tsiok tshenn, koh ū kuí-luā ia̍p ko-lê-tshài, tsi̍t kin ti-bah. <pause> guá bé láng, tsuán tńg--khì tshù suà-lo̍h sé tshài, tsú tsi̍t tiánn thng. Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, bûn kàu bī--lah, kóng a-má tsú ê thng siāng hó-tsia̍h. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g. Kiánn á kin sun-á uē siōng bô-thîng, tsiânn nāu sio honn.
