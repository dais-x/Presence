Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á kin sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, guá sing khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, hit kò bē tshài-thâu a-pô tsin hó-lé, i kóng kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì tsiok sio. Guá bé kuí-luā ia̍p tshài-thâu, tsi̍t kin ti-bah, nn̄g-bué sat-ba̍k-hî. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing tsú kip sun-á tsia̍h, suà--lo̍h-lâi sé-sann. Kiánn á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, guá tshiánn i tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsi̍t ka-jîn tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g--lah. Ta-ke kóng-uē tsin huann-hí, honn sun-á koh-kóng sueh a-má tsú ê thng siāng hó-tsia̍h. Kim-iā thinn-khì tsiok kuânn, m̄-thang pha̍k-sann--ah.
