Kin-á-ji̍t tsá á bô lo̍h-hōo, guá khí tī la̍k tiám àm-sî. A-bó kiò guá iau tsia̍h tsá-tshan, guá tō tsú tsi̍t tiánn lâi tsia̍h. lāi ū ke-nn̄g, tshài-thâu, ko-lê-tshài, hó îng-ióng. <pause> tsia̍h-pá liáu-āu khì biō kháu bé pò-tsuá, phòng-tio̍h tshù-pinn ang-bóo, i kóng thinn-khì tsin-hó, ta-ke suà-lo̍h to láng tshut-guā thài-iông. Guá huê tsū ka lâi, sun-á koh bu̍t tsuán-ha̍k hòng. A-bó sueh sun-á tso̍h--ji̍t ha̍k-hāu ū ua̍h-tāng, tann tsá iau tî-tò--ah. Guá kóng m̄-koh khuài khì pang sun-á tsún-pī su-pau, tshù lí tsi̍t-tsūn tsí tsin bâng--lah.
