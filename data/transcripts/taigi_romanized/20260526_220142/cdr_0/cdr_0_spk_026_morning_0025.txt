Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá khí-sin--lah, sing sé-bīn, koh-khì tsàu-kha tsú . Tsú hó --ah, suà-lo̍h tsia̍h-pn̄g. Sun-á koh buē khí, guá tsi̍t-lâng sing tsia̍h. Tsia̍h-pá pn̄g-āu, tsíng-lí mi̍h-kiānn, pé tshài-thâu, ko-lê-tshài tī ping-siunn hòng thò. Tsún-pī tshut-mn̂g tsiân, ná suànn láng bē-hiáu bô-iōng, thinn-khì khuànn lâi ē lo̍h-hōo. Kiânn--khì biō kháu bé kué liâu, ná ka bē kué liâu a-peh tsin hó-lé, kóng guá kin-á-ji̍t láng lâi tsia̍h-pá pn̄g. Koh tsuán tshù lâi--lah, sun-á iā khí-lâi, guá kà i sé-sann. <pause> sun-á thiann-tio̍h uē ba̍k koh táng, tshiò sueh iau o̍h, honn huann-hí.
