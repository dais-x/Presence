Kin-á-ji̍t tsá-thâu tsia̍h-pn̄g bô kuí sî, a-pah tī pâng-king hán guá, i kóng sueh guán ka lí tso̍h--ji̍t ū lâi Kheh-lâng. Hit kò sī guán tshù-pinn ê ang-bóo, lâi thàm bāng guán a-bó kiān-khong tāi-tsì. Guán a-bó m̄-koh tsuè-kīn kuí mê láng ū àm-mê tsâi khùn tio̍h, pe̍h thinn guán tō puê khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài. Tann tsá guán bé nn̄g kin tshenn-tshài, it-bué sat-ba̍k-hî, koh bé tsi̍t pau ko-lê-tshài. <pause> kiánn á kóng sueh guán sun-á tso̍h--ji̍t ha̍k-hāu pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, guán sun-á kóng a-má tsú ê thng siāng hó-tsia̍h. Guán thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué tsí tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
