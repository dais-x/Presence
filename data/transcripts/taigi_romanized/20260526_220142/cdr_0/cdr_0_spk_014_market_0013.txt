Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, hit kò bē soo-tshài o tsin hó-sim, i kóng kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì tsiok luán, tshenn-tshài tsiok tshenn-li̍k. Guá bé tsi̍t kin ko-lê-tshài, nn̄g kin tshài-thâu, koh bé tsi̍t nâ tsuí-kó. <pause> thâu-ke-niû sǹg khah sio̍k, kóng guá tī lâi tsia̍h-pn̄g, i huann-hí. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing sé tshài, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú tsi̍t tiánn thng. Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, kóng a-má tsú ê thng siāng hó-tsia̍h. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
