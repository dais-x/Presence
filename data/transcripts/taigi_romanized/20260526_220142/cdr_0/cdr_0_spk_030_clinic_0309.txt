Kin-á-ji̍t sī sann gue̍h tsa̍p sù hō, guá gōo tiám tō khí-tshn̂g--lah. <pause> thàu-tsá tsia̍h-pn̄g, guá tsú jī uánn tī tiān-ko, koh tsi̍t puânn tshài-thâu kuann, tsin kán-tan. A-bó kóng siāng hó-tsia̍h, tsia̍h hó to. Tńg--khì tsín-sóo sî, guá hê tio̍h tshài-tshī-á bé ê tang-sai, ū ti-bah, ke-nn̄g, ko-lê-tshài, thâu-ke-niû bē khah sio̍k, m̄-sī bē sio̍k--lah. Tsín-sóo lāi i-su mn̄g kin-ji̍t ji̍t-kî, guá tap sueh kin-á-ji̍t sī sann gue̍h tsa̍p sù hō, m̄ ē kóng tshò. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù āu, a-bó tsia̍h hó to thng, koh-kóng i tsin pá. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, siāng-huann-hí.
