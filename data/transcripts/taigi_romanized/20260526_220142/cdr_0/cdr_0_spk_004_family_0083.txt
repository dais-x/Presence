Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á kin sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, m̄-koh sing khì tshài-tshī-á bé tang-sai. <pause> guá kóng i guán tshù lí ū tio̍h kuí-luā ia̍p tshài-thâu, m̄ su-iàu tsài khì bé. Put-kò sin-pū kóng, tsuè-kīn thinn-khì tsin kuânn, iau to tsú sio-thng. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù--lah, sun-á tō pang-bâng sé tshài, kiánn á tsik sī khì sio-tsuí. Ta-ke to tsē tī tsàu-kha tán tsia̍h-pn̄g. A-bó kóng, kin-á-ji̍t iau tsò tiánn bah kenn, tsin bē lāu i guán tshù-lāi bô ti-bah. Guá thiann-tio̍h--ah, koh-khì bé tsi̍t kin ti-bah lâi. <pause> tíng tsá tsuán tńg--lâi--lah, tō khai-sí tsú thng, ta-ke tán ē tsí mā láng lâi tsia̍h. Sun-á kóng, a-má kin-á-ji̍t tsò ê kenn liāu tsin-hó, tsin bē lāu i guán tshù-lāi bô kuí-luā ia̍p tshài-thâu. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha pian tsia̍h-pn̄g--lah.
