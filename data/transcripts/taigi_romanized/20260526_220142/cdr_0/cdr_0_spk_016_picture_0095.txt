Kin-á-ji̍t khì biō kháu khuànn-hì kiánn á uán, tsia kò a-kong láng tsāi-tī biō-tiânn siāng khuànn-hì, hit kò a-pô tsin sio-sim, kóng-uē bē-thîng. <pause> guá khuànn-kàu tsi̍t tuì ang-bóo tshiú-khan-tshiú kiânn-lōo, kóng-uē--lah huann-hí. Hit kò tshài-tshī-á lâng tsē-tsē, sin-pū guán tshài-thâu, ko-lê-tshài, bé guán sat-ba̍k-hî, thâu-ke-niû kóng khah sio̍k. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing sé-sann, suà-lo̍h pha̍k-sann. Sun-á lâi uán, ná guán tsuí-kó tsia̍h, guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí. Tsi̍t ka-jîn tsē tī pâng-king tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
