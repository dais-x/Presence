Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá khí-lâi, sing khì sé-bīn, āu--lâi tō tsia̍h-pn̄g. Tann tsá a-bó tsú tshài-thâu thng, tsin hó-tsia̍h honn. Tsia̍h-pá liáu-āu, koh-khì pâng-king tsíng-lí mi̍h-kiānn, ná sann tshut-lâi pha̍k-sann. Tsún-pī iau khì tshài-tshī bé tshài, tńg--khì tsàu-kha láng ná tsînn-pau, io̍h sî, m̄ ē i lo̍k mi̍h sū. Tshut-mn̂g tsiân, sing kin a-pah kóng tsi̍t siann, i mā iā tsāi tsún-pī iau tshut-mn̂g, nn̄g tī tsi̍t khí-khì tshài-tshī-á--lah.
