Kin-á-ji̍t sī tsiann--gue̍h tsa̍p-gōo, a-bó kiò guá khì bé tsá-tshan. Guá thàu tsá-khí lâi-sé liám, liáu-āu ná tsînn khì tshài-tshī-á. Hit kò bē tshiam piánn ê thuann tsí pái láng tsāi biō kháu, guá kóng sueh i m̄-koh ū kuí to tshiam piánn, nā-sī tsin kín khih, guá ē-sái sing bé nn̄g pau. <pause> bē tshiam piánn a-pô tsiok hó-sim, kóng kin-á-ji̍t bô sīm lâng lâi bé, koh ē-sái ná khah-tsē tsînn. Guá bé nn̄g pau tshiam piánn, suà-lo̍h-khì tsín-sóo khuànn gê i. Tsín-sóo i-sing mn̄g guá kin-ji̍t kuí tsá-khí, guá tō kóng thàu-tsá--lah! I tsài mn̄g guá tsia̍h siánn tsá-tshan, guá tō kóng tsia̍h tshiam piánn. I-sing thiann-tio̍h , sueh kin-á-ji̍t sī guân-siau-tseh, iau tsia̍h thng înn. Nā guá m̄ ū bé thng înn, in-uī a-bó kiò guá khì bé tshiam piánn. Honn, tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, guá iau tsú tshiam piánn thng kip kiánn á tsia̍h, bián tī guā-bīn bé--ah.
