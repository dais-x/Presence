Guá kóng kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì hó, àm-sî bô lo̍h-hōo. <pause> guá tsa-hng ū tsú tsi̍t tiánn , ka ke-nn̄g tâng tshài-thâu, sun-á tsia̍h--lah huann-hí.
