Kin-á-ji̍t sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, a-bó sing tsú tsi̍t tiánn tshài-thâu bah kenn, tsin sio nāu. A-pah tī tsàu-kha pian tshiat tshài, kóng tio̍h ha̍k-hāu sū-bū, sun-á mā tsāi keh-piah pâng-king tsò kong-khò--lah. <pause> tsú hó āu, tsi̍t ke-hué tsí tsē tī tsàu-kha tsiân tsia̍h-pn̄g, kóng tio̍h hit kò sin-pū lâi pài-hóng uē-tê, honn huann-hí. Sun-á kóng a-má tsú ê tshài siāng hó-tsia̍h--ah, koh-kóng ha̍k-hāu huat-sing tshù-sū, ta-ke thiann-tio̍h tsin tshiò .
