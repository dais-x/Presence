Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á tâng sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian kóng-uē. A-bó sing sé kuí-luā ia̍p tshài-thâu, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú tsi̍t tiánn hî thng, tsin siāng-hó bī. Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--khì, kiàn kàu i a-má tsú ê thng, kóng "a-má tsú thng siāng hó-tsia̍h"--ah. Guá thiann-tio̍h koh tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ka-jîn tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu. Kiánn á kóng "a-kong kin-á-ji̍t ū láng lâi?" <pause> guá tap sueh "kin-á-ji̍t bô-khang îng, bîng ji̍t tsài tńg--lâi". Sun-á mn̄g "a-má, i guán ka tshài-thâu tsíng kuí to?" guá kóng "tshài-hn̂g lí tsíng-tio̍h hó to soo-tshài, ko-lê-tshài, tshài-thâu, kuà-tshài"--ah. Ta-ke pian tsia̍h-pn̄g pian kóng-uē, tsiânn huann-hí.
