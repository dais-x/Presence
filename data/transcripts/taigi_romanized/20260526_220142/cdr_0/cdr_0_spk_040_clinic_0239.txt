Kin-á-ji̍t tsá onn a-bó tio̍h-guá khùn kàu káu tiám. Tsuán lâi-sé liám tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsia̍h uánn tshing tah kiâm-tshài. <pause> thâu-ke-niû khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, kóng sueh ū lo̍h-hōo--ah, m̄ gî leh guā-thâu. Guá tann tsá bô tshut-mn̂g, tī tshù-lāi khuànn pò-tsuá. Tsín-sóo guán a-pah kiò guá khì niû-hueh-ap, kóng hueh-ap bē ko, m̄ su tam-sim tio̍h. Sun-á tso̍h--ji̍t hòng-ká tńg--lâi, tsò-hué tsia̍h-pn̄g. Tshài sī sat-ba̍k-hî tah lóo bah, koh ū tāu-hū tsú thng. Honn, tann tsá bô-siánn tāi-tsì.
