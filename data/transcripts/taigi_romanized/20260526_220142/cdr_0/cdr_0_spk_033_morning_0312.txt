Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá sing koh khùn khí, sé-bīn, suat gê, tio̍h sann--lah. Suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú , a-bó sueh hó-tsia̍h, koh-khì tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g--ah. Liáu-āu tsíng-lí mi̍h-kiānn, hê thâu-kin, tshiú-kin, io̍h sî, tshut-mn̂g khì tshài-tshī-á. Hit kò sin-pū tsin sio tsiânn, kóng guá ta̍k-pái láng lâi bé tshài. Tsuán huê tshù leh sing sé-uánn, suà--lo̍h-lâi pha̍k-sann, koh tsún-pī gōo tùn tsia̍h-pn̄g. <pause> hóo ū sun-á lâi sueh iau pang-bâng tsú tsia̍h, guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī pâng-king tsia̍h gōo-tshan, tsiânn sio nāu honn.
