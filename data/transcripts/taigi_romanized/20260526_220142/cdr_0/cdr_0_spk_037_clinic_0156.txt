Kin-á-ji̍t àm-sî guá tsia̍h tsá , tsin nńg , ka kuí kháu ke-nn̄g, tsiok îng-ióng. Tsia̍h uân āu tńg--khì tsín-sóo khuànn i-sing, kóng guá kin-á-ji̍t tsia̍h siánn-iūnn ê tsá-tshan? Guá tō kóng "tsia̍h tsá --lah". <pause> hit kò i-sing iū mn̄g guá tsuè-kīn ka lí ū-bô huat-sing siánn tāi-tsì? Guá tō kóng sueh "bô-siánn tsiânn sū--lah". I-sing tsài mn̄g sueh kin-á-ji̍t kuí hō? Guá tō tap "kin-á-ji̍t sī sing-kî gōo".--ah, i-sing mn̄g guá lín to būn-tê, guá to tsi̍t-kù kù tap tio̍h.
