Kin-á-ji̍t tsá sîn gōo tiám khí, sing tsú tsi̍t tiánn hi pn̄g tâng kuí uánn tshài-thâu thng. Sun-á tsa-hng bé-lâi kò ke-nn̄g, tsiânn tsi̍t puânn tshá ke-nn̄g. <pause> tsia̍h uân tsá-tshan āu, tshù-pinn a-má lâi pài bāng, kóng guán ka kin-ji̍t tsin an siông. Guá kóng sī--ooh, m̄-koh tann tsá ū khì pēnn-īnn tsín-sóo khuànn i-sing, in-uī thâu-thiànn hó to thinn. Tsín-sóo i-sing mn̄g guán kin-ji̍t kuí hō, guá kóng put tsai, koh mn̄g a-bó tō tsai. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù--lah, sun-á kiò guá khì tsia̍h gōo tùn pn̄g, tsin huann-hí.
