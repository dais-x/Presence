Kin-á-ji̍t sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, hit kò kiánn á iā mā láng lâi, tsin sio nāu. Guá sing khì tshī-tiûnn bé tshài, tī biō kháu pōng kàu a-kong, i mn̄g sueh guán kin-ji̍t tsú siánn? <pause> guá kóng iau tsú thng pá, īng sin-sian soo-tshài tâng ti-bah, siāng hó-liāu. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù lâi, sing sé tshài tsài tshiat bah, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsàu-kha tsú thng. Thng tsú hó liáu-āu, ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsin huann-hí. Sun-á kóng i tsia̍h-kàu a-má tsú ê thng, honn tsiok-hó tsu-bī. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsū tsāi tsi̍t-tsê kóng-uē, tsiânn sio nāu.
