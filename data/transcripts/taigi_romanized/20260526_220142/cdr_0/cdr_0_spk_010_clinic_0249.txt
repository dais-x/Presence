Kin-á-ji̍t sī gue̍h tshe-it, a-bó kiò kiánn á tsá khí-khì bé tshài. Guá sing tsia̍h uánn tsò tsá-tshan, tsài tsú kò ke-nn̄g-thng. <pause> haih, thinn-khì hó im, bē lo̍h-hōo, nā àm-sî ē kuânn. Tńg--lâi tshù liáu-āu, sun-á kóng iau khì kong-hn̂g kiânn-lōo, guá tō kin i tsi̍t khí-khì. Huán lâi āu, a-bó mn̄g guá ū-bô tsia̍h-pá, guá tap sueh tsia̍h-pá--ah, tsin huann-hí. Gōo pn̄g sī tshài-thâu bah thng phuè-pn̄g, koh ū hî kuann, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī toh pian tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
