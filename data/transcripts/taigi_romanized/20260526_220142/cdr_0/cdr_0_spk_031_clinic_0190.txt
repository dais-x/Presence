Kin-á-ji̍t thàu tsá-khí kò lâi tsia̍h-pn̄g, guá ū tsú nn̄g uánn kin kuí tshài-thâu láng tsi̍t-tè lâi tsú. Guá m̄-sī to̍k-tsū tsia̍h, a-bó tī keh-piah pâng-king, i tńg--lâi sueh iau tâng guá tsi̍t-tsê tsia̍h. <pause> guá mn̄g i tsa-hng ū-bô tsia̍h hó? A-bó kóng sueh tsia̍h siōng hó, m̄ tsai kin-á-ji̍t siūnn tsia̍h siánn. Guá piān sueh kin-ji̍t tsú nn̄g-bué sió hî lâi póo-siông tso̍h--ji̍t. Koh sé-sann pha̍k tshut-khì, sun-á khì ha̍k-hāu--ah, guá tńg--lâi tshù-pinn biō kháu bé kuí tshài-thâu kin bah. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù tiong liáu-āu, sing tsú tsi̍t tiánn thng, thng ū ka kuí ia̍p tshenn-tshài, tsin siāng-hó bī. M̄-koh a-bó koh siūnn tsia̍h tiám kuann pn̄g, guá tō tsài tshue kip i tsia̍h. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsê tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsin huann-hí.
