Kin-á-ji̍t guá khì khuànn biō kháu ê tshài-tshī-á, khuànn tī hit kò thuann-uī koh ū a-kong bē tāu-kuann, i kóng tāu-kuann tsiok hiunn. <pause> guá bé kuí tè, koh bé tsi̍t kin tāu-hū, kuí ia̍p ko-lê-tshài. Thâu-ke-niû sǹg khah sio̍k, kóng kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì tsiok-hó, ē-sái lâi bé tshài. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing sé-sann, suà--lo̍h-lâi pha̍k-sann. Sun-á kià su-pau lâi, kóng a-má kin-á-ji̍t tsiok khîn la̍t. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī pâng-king ím-tê, tsiânn huan-him.
