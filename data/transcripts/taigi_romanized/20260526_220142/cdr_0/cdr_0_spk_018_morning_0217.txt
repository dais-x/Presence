Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá sing khí-tshn̂g, koh-khì tsàu-kha sé-bīn. Sé uân--ah, tsuán tī pâng-king tshīng sann. Tshīng thò--lah, tńg--lâi tsàu-kha tsú pn̄g tsia̍h. Tsia̍h-pá āu, tsíng-lí mi̍h-kiānn, hòng hó tsi̍t láng. <pause> suà-lo̍h ná pau-pau, io̍h sî, tsún-pī tshut-mn̂g. A-bó kóng guá bē iau bē-kì-tit hê tài hōo-suànn, bián-tit lo̍h-hōo sî ē-sái khùn-jiáu. Thiann-tio̍h tsin tuì. Tńg--khì tuā mn̂g, khé-tōng tiān-thui. Hā-lâu liáu-āu, tshut-guā kiânn-lōo, siāng tshài-tshī-á bé tshài. Tshài-tshī-á lâng tsè, bé nn̄g kin ko-lê-tshài, it-bué sat-ba̍k-hî, koh kuí tè bah. Thâu-ke-niû kè-kàu sio̍k, kóng guá ē-sái láng lâi bé khah-tsē. Tsuán huê tshù--ah, sé-sann pha̍k-sann. Sun-á huán o̍h sî, kóng a-kong tsú ê thng siāng hó-tsia̍h, tsin huann-hí. Tsi̍t ka-jîn tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
