Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá tsia̍h piān sî m̄-sī kuí sî--ah, guá tsia̍h tsi̍t-tǹg tah nn̄g lia̍p ke-nn̄g. Sun-á lâi hán guá khí-tshn̂g, kóng tshù-pinn a-pô kip tī tshài-thâu, tsin-tsē. Tńg--khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, hit kò bē-bah ê ang kóng kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì hó, bô lo̍h-hōo. Guá bé kuí kin ti-bah, ko-lê-tshài, ia̍p tshài-thâu. <pause> tsuán tńg--khì tshù lâi sing sé-sann, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú tsi̍t tiánn hî thng. Sun-á tńg--lâi tsia̍h piān sî, kóng thng siāng hó-tsia̍h. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu.
