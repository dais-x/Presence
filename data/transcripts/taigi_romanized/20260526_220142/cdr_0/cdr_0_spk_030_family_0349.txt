Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, sing khì tshài-tshī-á bé kuí-luā ia̍p tshài-thâu, tsi̍t kin ti-bah, <pause> tông-sî bē bōng láng tī hî thuann bé nn̄g-bué sat-ba̍k-hî. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù lâi, i sing sé-tsīng tshài-thâu tâng ti-bah, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú tiánn thng. Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, bûn kàu thng bī, kóng i hó siūnn tsia̍h. A-kong thiann-tio̍h, tshiò sueh: "tán-tsi̍t-ē tō hó, thng koh tī hué siāng sio." sun-á koh m̄ thîng tshui-tshiok, tsi̍t ka-jîn tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsin huann-hí--ah.
