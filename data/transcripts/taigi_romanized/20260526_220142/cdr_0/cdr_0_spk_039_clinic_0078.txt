Kin-á-ji̍t tshing sîn tsia̍h tāu-tsiunn iû-tiâu, bī jî tsin-hó. Tsa-hng a-bó tsú tshài-thâu-sng thng, guá tsia̍h bē pá, tann tsá tsia̍h to tsi̍t-tiám--ah. Tńg--khì tsín-sóo, i-su mn̄g guá ji̍t-kî, kóng sī sì-gue̍h jia̍p la̍k. <pause> i-su tsài mn̄g guá tsá-tshan tsia̍h siánn, guá tap sī tāu-tsiunn iû-tiâu. I mn̄g ka lí huat-sing siánn tāi-tsì, guá kóng sun-á huán o̍h, a-kong sin-thé bô , tsi̍t ka tsí to hó. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, a-bó lâi kóng tshài-tshī-á hî tsiok tshenn, iau guá khì bé it-bué sat-ba̍k-hî, tsi̍t kin ti-bah. Guá koh iau khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, liáu-āu tsuán tńg--khì tshù tsú pn̄g tsia̍h-pn̄g--lah.
