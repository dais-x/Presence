Kin-á-ji̍t sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, hit kò kiánn á iā lâi--lah. Ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian kóng-uē, sun-á kóng ha̍k-hāu lí huat-sing ê sū, a-kong thiann-tio̍h huann-hí, kóng í-tsîng tha̍k-tsu sî tsin khik-khóo. Bóo khì tshài-tshī-á bé kuí-luā ia̍p tshài-thâu, ke-nn̄g, tsài tsiá tō tsú tsi̍t tiánn bah kenn. Sun-á kóng i ài tsia̍h bah kenn, kóng i m̄-sī kiánn á--lah, ē tsia̍h tuā-lâng si̍t-bu̍t--lah. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn sio nāu--ah.
