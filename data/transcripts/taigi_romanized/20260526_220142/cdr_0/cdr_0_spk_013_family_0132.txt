Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á tâng sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, a-pah sing khì tshī-tiûnn bé tshài, bé it-bué sat-ba̍k-hî, koh bé kuí kin ti-bah, kuí pau soo-tshài. Tsuán tńg--lâi liáu-āu, a-má sé-tsīng tshài, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú thng tâng tshá-tshài. <pause> thng tsiok siāng hó-tsia̍h, sun-á kóng i tsia̍h láng beh pá--ah. Tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha pian tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsin huann-hí. A-kong uē kóng kin-ji̍t tsin-hó thinn-khì, ta-ke to mā khai-sim--lah.
