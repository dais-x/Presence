Siàu-liân sî-tsūn, guá muí ji̍t tha̍k-tsheh khì ha̍k-hāu. A-pah tī tshân lí lông-bâng, a-bó koh tsò ka-bū. Hit kò ji̍t-tsí tsin pîng-si̍t. Hòng-ká sî-tseh, a-kong kiò guá khì pang kong. Guá khì tshân lí o̍h tsìng-tshài, iā o̍h kuah tiū. Gōo āu hiu sik, tsē tī tshù-pinn tsio̍h-thâu siāng tsia̍h piān-tong, kóng-uē--lah thiann-lâng-kóng uē. Huán lâi ha̍k-hāu--ah, sai-hū kà tha̍k-tsheh, siá-jī. Hòng sú-ká--lah, kin kiánn á khì khe-pinn hì tsuí. A-má tsú hó to hó-tsia̍h ê tshài kip lán tsia̍h. Hòng-ká liáu-āu, m̄-sī muí ji̍t liām-tsu, koh ū-sî-kan tsūn pîng-iú uán. Ná sî tsin huann-hí, sing-ua̍h kán-tan khiok tsiok ū tshù. honn, kin-á-ji̍t siūnn-khí-lâi koh ē tshiò.
