Kin-á-ji̍t sī la̍k-gue̍h peh hō, tsa-hng àm-sî bô lo̍h-hōo, thinn-khì tsin-hó. Thàu-tsá guá tsia̍h tsi̍t-tǹg , ka kuí lia̍p ke-nn̄g kin tshài-thâu thng. Tńg--khì tsín-sóo, i-sing mn̄g guá lín án-tsuánn, kóng lâi khuànn-pēnn. <pause> guá tap sueh kin-á-ji̍t bô-siánn tāi-tsì, a-kong tī tshù, sun-á khì ha̍k-hāu. Tsá-tshan ū tsia̍h tinn tāu-hue, tsin sūn kháu. Honn, tsuè-kīn bô lo̍h-hōo, tshài kè khah sio̍k, bé kuí kin ko-lê-tshài, hòng tio̍h bān-bān tsia̍h. Ka lí it-tshè pîng-an, m̄ siánn tsiânn sū thé.
