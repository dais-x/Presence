Kin-á-ji̍t thàu-tsá khí-lâi, sing khì sé-bīn, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú tsi̍t tiánn hi pn̄g tâng nn̄g puânn-tshài. Koh-khì tsàu-kha ná uánn , tsíng-lí tsi̍t-ē tshù-lāi mi̍h-kiānn. Sun-á sueh ē tńg--lâi tsia̍h tsá-tshan, guá tsún-pī hó tō tán tio̍h hit kò kiánn á. <pause> sun-á tńg--lâi leh, tsē tī toh tsiân tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsin huann-hí. Sun-á kóng a-má tsú ê tsiok tinn, koh-kóng tshài-thâu thng siāng-hó ím. Tsia̍h uân pn̄g, suà-lo̍h tsíng-lí uánn-puânn, tsún-pī khì tshài-tshī bé tshài. Lîng tio̍h nâ tsí kiânn-lōo khì biō kháu tshài-tshī, bé hî-bah tâng tshài-thâu tńg--khì. Tsuán tńg--khì tshù āu, koh iau sé-sann pha̍k-sann, tsún-pī tshut-khì ē tshù-pinn uē ka-siông.
