Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á láng tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, sing khì tshài-tshī-á bé tshài, bé it-bué sat-ba̍k-hî, koh bé tsi̍t kin ti-bah, kuí ia̍p ko-lê-tshài. Tńg--khì tshù liáu-āu, sing sé tshài, suà--lo̍h-lâi tsú nn̄g tiánn thng. Sun-á pàng-o̍h tńg--lâi, kóng a-má tsú ê thng tsin hó-tsia̍h. Guá thiann-tio̍h tsin huann-hí, tsi̍t ke-hué-á tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsiânn nāu sio--ah. Sun-á mn̄g guá sueh kin-ji̍t thinn-khì án-tsuánn? Guá sueh àm-sî lo̍h-hōo, kin-á-ji̍t tshut ji̍t-thâu, thinn-khì put tshò honn. Ta-ke pian tsia̍h-pn̄g pian liâu-thian, kóng-khí í-tsîng kòo-sū, tsin ū tshù.
