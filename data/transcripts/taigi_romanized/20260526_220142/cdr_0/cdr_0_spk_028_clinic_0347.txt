Kin-á-ji̍t a-kong lâi tī tshù, guá láng m̄ khì tshài-tshī-á. It-tsá khí-lâi tō tsia̍h , ka kuí tè bah kuann. <pause> a-bó kóng tshài-thâu tsin-hó bī, lán nn̄g kò-jîn tsē tī toh-thâu tsia̍h-pn̄g. Sun-á buē tńg--lâi, a-kong sueh kin-á-ji̍t thinn-khì , m̄ sik-ha̍p tshut-khì kiânn-lōo. Guá thiann-tio̍h , kóng sueh tō lâu tsāi tshù-lāi khuànn tiān-sī. Àm-sî khì pēnn-īnn tsò kiám-tsa, i-sing mn̄g kin-ji̍t kuí hō? Guá kóng sī tsa̍p-it gue̍h jia̍p sann hō. <pause> i-su mn̄g tsá-tshan tsia̍h siánn? Guá kóng tann tsá tsia̍h phuè bah kuann, m̄ ū tsia̍h-tshài. Sun-á bô lâi, a-kong tō tsū-kí tsú pn̄g kip i tsia̍h.
