Kin-á-ji̍t kiánn á tâng sun-á tńg--lâi tshù-lāi tsia̍h-pn̄g, ta-ke tsē tī tsàu-kha pian kóng-uē. A-kong khì bé tshài huán , kóng tshài-tshī-á tsin láng muá-jîn. Guá tsú tsi̍t tiánn tshài-thâu bah kenn, koh tshá kuí ia̍p ko-lê-tshài. <pause> sun-á kóng i ha̍k-hāu o̍h sin-ti sik, kóng sòo-ha̍k tê-bo̍k lān. Kiánn á mā kóng kong-si sing-ì hó, kóng thâu-ke ē-sái tshiánn sin tông-sū. Tsi̍t ka-jîn tsē tī tsàu-kha tsia̍h-pn̄g, tsin sio nāu--ah. A-kong kóng tshù-lāi huann-hí khuànn-kàu ta-ke tsū tsi̍t-tsê, him uì koh sim-an--lah.
